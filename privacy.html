CloudNow

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 10, 2026

CloudNow is an independent app that lets you access your GeForce NOW account on Apple TV. This policy explains what data the app handles and how.

Data We Do Not Collect

CloudNow does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to any server operated by the developer. There are no analytics, crash reporting services, or advertising SDKs in the app.

Authentication

CloudNow uses NVIDIA's official OAuth device flow to authenticate your GeForce NOW account. Your login credentials are entered directly on NVIDIA's website and are never seen or stored by CloudNow. The resulting access token is stored locally on your Apple TV using the system Keychain and is used solely to communicate with NVIDIA's GeForce NOW services.

Data Transmitted to Third Parties

To provide its core functionality, CloudNow communicates with NVIDIA's GeForce NOW servers. This communication is subject to NVIDIA's own privacy policy. CloudNow does not share your data with any other third party.

Local Storage

CloudNow stores the following data locally on your device only:

- Your GeForce NOW authentication token (Keychain)

- Your in-app preferences such as stream quality settings and favorites (UserDefaults)

This data never leaves your device except as required to communicate with NVIDIA's services.

Children's Privacy

CloudNow does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The app does not contain any features directed at children.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised date.

Contact
